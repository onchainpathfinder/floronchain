About

I'm Floriana — an AML professional and crypto enthusiast since 2017. I work at the intersection of financial crime prevention and digital asset innovation.

My focus areas include compliance frameworks for crypto businesses, crypto crime investigation, onchain analysis, and the evolving regulatory landscape across the EU and beyond.

I'm deeply passionate about education and training — because the best defence against financial crime is people who understand what they're looking at.

Whether you're a compliance officer trying to understand DeFi, a crypto native who wants to stay on the right side of regulation, or just someone curious about how digital assets actually work — I'm writing this for you.

This site is a space for clear thinking about crypto, compliance, and the future of digital finance. No hype, no FUD — just practical insights from someone who lives in both worlds.
