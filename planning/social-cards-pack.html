HOW TO CUSTOMISE THESE PROMPTS

Swap the vibe: Replace "editorial" with "minimalist", "retro", "3D render", or "watercolor" to change the entire feel.

Change colours: Replace colour descriptions like "coral and gold" with your brand colours. E.g., "teal and navy" for floronchain.

Add your location: Add "Mediterranean setting" or "Malta coastline" or "Valletta streets" to make it personal.

For Midjourney: Keep the --ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6 at the end. Change --ar to 16:9 for blog banners.

For DALL-E / ChatGPT: Remove the -- flags at the end. Just use the description text.

For Canva AI: Shorten to 1-2 sentences. E.g., "Flat lay of healthy meal prep containers, bold colors, overhead shot"
